golden ratio: (1+root(5)) / 2
conjugate: (1-root(5)) / 2

phi^2 = (1+2root(5) + 5)/4
      =	1.5 + root(5)/2 

1+phi = 1/2 + root(5)/2 + 1 
      = 1.5 + root(5)/2

phi^2 = 1 + phi






phiHat^2 = (1-2root(5)+5)/4
         = 1.5 - root(5)/2
phiHat + 1 = .5 - root(5)/2 + 1
           = 1.5 - root(5)/2

phiHat^2 = 1 + phiHat
